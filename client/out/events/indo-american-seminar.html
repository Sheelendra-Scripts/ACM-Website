About This Event

The GGSIPU EDC ACM & USS ACM-W Student Chapters organized an enlightening speaker session on 'Education Opportunities in Higher Education ‚Äì Indo-American Linkages.' The session was delivered by Dr. Ron Buckmire, Dean of the School of Computer Science & Mathematics at Marist University, New York. Dr. Buckmire shared valuable insights into international academic collaborations, research prospects, and pathways for higher education in the US. The interactive session guided students on navigating the evolving landscape of global education and emphasized the importance of interdisciplinary learning in the era of AI and automation.
